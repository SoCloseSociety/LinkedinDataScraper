## UI Component Styles

### Buttons (Streamlit/Web)
- **Primary**: `#575ECF` background, white text, 6px radius
- **Hover**: `#7B80E0` background
- **Disabled**: `#3a3a3a` background, `#666` text

### Cards / Panels
- **Background**: `#1e1e1e` or `#f8f9fa` (light mode)
- **Border**: 1px `#575ECF` at 20% opacity
- **Border Radius**: 8px
- **Shadow**: `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`

### Data Tables
- **Header**: `#575ECF` background, white text
- **Alt Rows**: `#F2F4F8`
- **Hover Row**: `#575ECF` at 8% opacity
- **Links**: `#575ECF` underlined
